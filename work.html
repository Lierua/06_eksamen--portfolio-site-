02_WEB

During this theme, I learned to use the fundamental tools essential for a multimedia designer's work. These skills formed the foundation for the rest of my education.

I worked with design principles for digital user interfaces and gained experience in creating digital content, as well as communicating and designing for various platforms. I learned how to build web pages using HTML and CSS, which provided me with an understanding of responsive web design. Additionally, I became familiar with graphic design and photo editing in Photoshop, and I worked on setting up text and images in Figma.

03_UX/UI

During this theme, I gained experience with selected UX/UI methods and learned how to present my process for designing and developing products and solutions. I also worked on communicating my research and test results to stakeholders in a clear and effective manner.

04_ANIMATION

During this theme, I was introduced to the programming language JavaScript, which I used to code dynamic websites. In this project, I combined JavaScript with CSS animations to create a small game. The game was based on a concept and idea that I developed myself, and I designed all elements and coded the game entirely from scratch.

I worked professionally with the creation of graphical elements and analyzed game design to understand how to engage users through theory and conventions related to UI elements, character design, background design, and composition techniques. Additionally, I developed my own game design following modern conventions and drew the graphics for the game entirely from scratch in Adobe Illustrator.

05_VIDEO

During this theme, I gained a foundational understanding of content production, which included pre-production, production, and post-production. I worked with smartphone cameras for video recording and external audio, and I used Premiere Pro and Audition for editing. This gave me the skills to independently produce small-scale content and enhanced my ability to communicate professionally with clients.

I also worked with animated vector graphics (LottieFiles) and was introduced to fundamental concepts in content production. Furthermore, I applied the skills I had acquired in previous themes to redesign a company website.
